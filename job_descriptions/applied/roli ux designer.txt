We are looking for a talented mid-weight UX Designer to create exquisite user experiences. You should have an eye for clean and artful design, possess exceptional UI skills, be able to translate high-level requirements into interaction flows and artefacts, and transform them into beautiful, intuitive, and functional user interfaces. This is a great opportunity to be part of a talented team with an aim to reinvent music making.

Please note that we require a portfolio as part of your application. We also recommend reading our helpful tips on how to make your application stand out before applying.

 

Your Responsibilities:

Collaborate closely with our software team and other disciplines across the company to deliver elegant solutions to complex workflows
Work with a Scrum Development team to implement and refine designs
Collaborate with Product Management and UI Designers to define and implement creative solutions for the product direction, visuals, and experience
Take the initiative in providing input and feedback in cross-team discussions
Develop high level and/or detailed storyboards, mockups and prototypes to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas
Present the noble UX ideas to the wider team
Gauge the usability of new and existing products and making constructive suggestions for change
Be the user-advocate during the development process, subjecting early-stage designs to usability testing or expert review, and offering implementation suggestions from a user-centred perspective
Use research-related services, theories, and methods to support your recommendations
Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback
 

Your Qualifications:

At least 3 years of hands-on experience and a proven record with implemented features in a software product design, interaction design, or user experience design role
Excellent UX design skills with a strong portfolio
Experience working across the full user centred design process, including user research and testing
Experience working in an Agile/Scrum development process
Ability to work in team
Deep knowledge of a variety of UX methodologies and frameworks
Up-to-date knowledge with the latest UI trends, techniques, and technologies
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
Ability to present your designs and sell your solutions to various stakeholders
Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, or other visual design and rapid prototyping tools of the likes of InVision, Marvel, Flinto, Framer.js, Keynote, etc
Ideal Qualifications:

Pair/team design experience
Experience using Audio Software with understanding of common workflows to play and make music
Familiar with Game Design techniques that could apply to consumer products
