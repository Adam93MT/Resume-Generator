Job description
Designing at Mix mean designing from the beginning. We are looking for an experienced Product Designer to join our team and help craft our product set. This will include our website, mobile applications and plugins. We are looking for designers who are passionate about solving problems and telling product stories. The work is highly cross-functional and will require you to collaborate closely with engineering, product, design, and future hires. We value designers who like showcasing their experience working across interaction, UI, and visual design.

Responsibilities

Collaborate cross-functionally with product management, engineering and user research to ensure a smooth, data-driven product development process
Design and prototype elegant and highly useful flows and experiences across multiple media
Guide the team to develop best practices for ensuring a high caliber of quality
Contribute to high-level strategic decisions with the rest of the product and executive teams
Execute in a fast-paced and highly fluid startup environment where thought and initiative are valued


Required Qualifications

5+ years experience building products and features for mobile and desktop (native or web)
Passion for creating simple products that people use every day
Humble, hard-working, with an enthusiasm for learning
Strong portfolio with well-articulated examples of visual design and interaction design across mobile and web applications
Experience with high-level product strategy
Ability to empathize with different kinds of users
Excellent communication skills and attention to detail
Exceptional organizational and analytical skills
Proficient in Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, or other design tools
Basic HTML/CSS & Javascript knowledge; current on latest responsive design techniques


Desired Qualifications

Prior experience in a startup
Background working in consumer media
Proficiency in HTML, CSS and JavaScript
Experience using prototyping tools such as Flinto, InVision, Pixate, FramerJS, etc.
Experience running and consuming UX research
Experience with branding and marketing
Obsession with typography
